# Vietnamese Text Guidelines

Vietnamese image text is fragile. Keep it short and explicit.

## Rules

- Use correct accents.
- Prefer 2-5 words per label.
- Avoid long sentences inside the image.
- Use one clear headline and short labels.
- Do not use too many numbers unless the source requires them.
- If prices or dates appear in source content, copy exactly.
- For business audiences, use practical language and avoid hype.

## Good Labels

- `Duyệt hồ sơ`
- `Đào tạo chuyên đề`
- `Group Coaching`
- `Triển khai DN`
- `Minh bạch phí`

## Risky Labels

- Long claims with many clauses
- Dense value propositions
- Legal or pricing disclaimers
- Text with too many abbreviations
