# Cover Spec (WEC-class Minimal Covers)

For article, blog, event, and playbook covers: only a title, a subtitle, and abstract decorative motifs. No scenes, no characters, no content keypoints on the image.

Reference template: `assets/WEC-Watercolor-Editorial-Cover-template.png`

## Composition Zones (16:9 base)

- **Text zone**: horizontal center band, roughly 60% of width, vertically centered. Motifs must never sit behind text.
- **Title**: the largest element. 1-2 lines, maximum 8 words.
- **Subtitle**: 1 line under the title, maximum 12 words, smaller and lighter than the title.
- **Optional meta line**: a short date or series name below the subtitle, smallest text.
- **Motifs**: corners and edges only, fading toward the center.
- **Signature**: bottom corner (see Signature section).

## Text Budget

- Title ≤ 8 words. Subtitle ≤ 12 words. Nothing else except the optional meta line and the signature.
- Vietnamese text follows `vietnamese-text-guidelines.md`. Accents must be exact — spell out the full title and subtitle verbatim in the prompt.

## Abstract Motif Library

Pick 1-2 motif families per cover, never more:

- Watercolor washes, soft color blooms
- Botanical edges: leaves, thin branches
- Ink splashes, dry brush strokes
- Soft geometry: thin circles, fine lines, light grids
- Paper texture, torn or deckled edges
- Small still-life accent (coffee cup, pen, notebook) only when the content calls for it

Avoid: literal objects illustrating the topic, people, mascots, busy scenes, photo elements.

## Palette

Default WEC palette: warm white paper, soft blue + gray + cream washes, navy headline ink.

A variant palette may be derived from content mood, but keep at most 3 hues plus paper white, all soft and desaturated.

## Aspect Variants

- `16:9` — default; shared across blog, slide, and social.
- `4:5`, `1:1` — on request only. Keep the same center text-zone logic when the ratio changes.

## Negative List

Always include in the prompt: no extra text, no placeholder text, no people or characters, no busy scene, no logo unless provided, no heavy gradients, no tiny unreadable text, no clutter near the text zone.

## Signature

- `#voquoccuong` placed at a bottom corner (bottom-right by default).
- Small, low-contrast, slightly faded ink — a quiet signature mark, never competing with the title.
- Omit only when the user explicitly asks for no signature or provides their own brand mark.
