# Style Library

Human overview of the seven styles. Machine-readable specs with exact palette hex, typography, composition, density limits, and failure modes live in `styles/{CODE}.yaml` — read the YAML of the selected style before writing the final prompt.

## ACD - AI Contrast Drama

Template: `assets/ACD-AI-Contrast-Drama-template.jpg`

Use for hooks, covers, thumbnails, before/after, human vs AI, manual vs automation.

Style: cinematic split-screen, photorealistic, dramatic. Left side shows manual, chaotic, warm orange. Right side shows AI, system, blue-cyan. Minimal text.

## WEC - Watercolor Editorial Cover

Template: `assets/WEC-Watercolor-Editorial-Cover-template.png`

Use for article covers, event recaps, playbook covers, calm premium banners.

Style: elegant watercolor on warm white paper. Soft blue, gray, and cream washes. Large clean headline, minimal text, decorative edges.

For full composition, text budget, motif, palette, and signature rules, follow `references/cover-spec.md`.

## SBS - Sketchnote Blue Story

Template: `assets/SBS-Sketchnote-Blue-Story-template.jpg`

Use for one message, one insight, quote, story, reflective lesson.

Style: blue ballpoint sketch on white paper, handwritten typography, lots of whitespace. Use one central metaphor only. Navy blue ink.

## SB - Sketchnote Blue

Template: `assets/SB-Sketchnote-Blue-template.jpg`

Use for multiple lessons, bullet points, recap, list of ideas.

Style: blue ballpoint notebook page, hand-drawn sketches. Title at top, 3-6 bullets with metaphor icons. Navy blue ink.

## FBW - FlowBot Watercolor

Template: `assets/FBW-FlowBot-Watercolor-template.jpg`

Use for workflow, SOP, step-by-step process, automation flow, tutorial.

Style: friendly AI guide, soft watercolor, educational. Horizontal left-to-right workflow with arrows. Blue/cream with orange/yellow accents.

## BLD - Business Line Diagram

Template: `assets/BLD-Business-Line-Diagram-Minimal-template.png`

Use for framework, system, architecture, strategy map, decision tree, org chart.

Style: minimal black line art on white. Boxes, arrows, connectors, simple icons. 95% black/white, max 5% orange accent.

## VTK - Visual Thinking Knowledge

Template: `assets/VTK-Visual-Thinking-Knowledge-template.jpg`

Use for deep analysis, visual essay, knowledge map, complex concept.

Style: hand-drawn black ink knowledge map. Top headline, central insight, 3-7 connected branches. Text can be 50-70% when the user needs a detailed map. Black/white with one accent.
