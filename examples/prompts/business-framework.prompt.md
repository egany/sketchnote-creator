# Example Prompt: Business Framework

Selected style: BLD - Business Line Diagram.

Create a 16:9 minimal business line diagram on warm white paper. Use black hand-drawn line art with one small orange accent. Show a clean operating system map labeled `Operating System for AI Adoption`.

Layout: left-to-right flow with five connected blocks: `Strategy`, `Process`, `People`, `Tools`, `Measurement`. Place `Tools` after `Process` to show that tools should not come first. Add a small lever icon near `Process` and a dashboard icon near `Measurement`.

Text inside image: `Strategy`, `Process`, `People`, `Tools`, `Measurement`, `Tools amplify the workflow you already have`.

Avoid photorealism, gradients, clutter, long paragraphs, tiny labels, and decorative icons without meaning.
