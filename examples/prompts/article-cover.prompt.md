# Example Prompt: Article Cover

Selected style: WEC - Watercolor Editorial Cover (per `references/cover-spec.md`).

Create a 16:9 elegant watercolor editorial cover on warm white paper, matching the WEC template style.

Layout: center text band, roughly 60% of width. Title in large navy hand-lettered ink across 1-2 lines: `Stop Buying AI Tools`. Subtitle in smaller, lighter ink below: `Fix your workflow first`. Small meta line beneath the subtitle in faint gray: `A guide for business owners`.

Motifs: soft blue and cream watercolor washes in the corners fading toward the center, plus a thin botanical branch along the top-left edge. Maximum 2 motif families. Nothing behind the text zone.

Palette: warm white paper, soft blue, gray, cream. Navy ink for the title only.

Signature: `#voquoccuong` at the bottom-right corner, small, low-contrast faded ink.

Reproduce all text exactly as written, including capitalization. Avoid: extra text, placeholder text, people, characters, busy scenes, logos, heavy gradients, tiny unreadable text, clutter near the text zone.
